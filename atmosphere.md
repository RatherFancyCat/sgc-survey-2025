## Key Findings Summary

<div class="alert alert-warning" markdown="1">

### Critical Insights

1. **SGC operates as 20 mini-communities, not 1 unified community**
   - In-clan: 4.3/5
   - Cross-clan: 3.2/5
   - Gap of 1.1 points is significant

2. **Members love their clan, tolerate SGC**
   - Individual clan: 4.4/5 (HIGHEST)
   - Overall SGC: 3.5/5
   - Gap of 0.9 points

3. **Elitism and division are real problems**
   - Repeatedly mentioned across feedback
   - Members avoid cross-clan spaces
   - "Mega-clan" concept not functioning as intended

4. **Most clans thrive internally**
   - Only a few (Rise, partially Fury) show internal atmosphere issues
   - Problem is not individual clan management
   - Problem is community-wide unity

5. **Competitive events harm atmosphere**
   - Clan Wars specifically mentioned as divisive
   - Created hostility between clans
   - Collaborative events likely better fit

</div>

## Recommendations

<div class="card" markdown="1">

### To Improve Cross-Clan Atmosphere

1. **Address Elitism Directly**
   - Clear community standards
   - Enforcement of respectful behavior across clans
   - Leadership modeling inclusive behavior

2. **Create Cross-Clan Opportunities**
   - More collaborative events (not competitive)
   - Open voice channels in each clan
   - Cross-clan mentorship programs

3. **Reduce Structural Barriers**
   - Standardize activity requirements across clans
   - Make multi-gaming count toward activity
   - Encourage clan-hopping for events

4. **Emphasize "One SGC" Identity**
   - Reinforce mega-clan concept
   - Celebrate cross-clan successes
   - Reduce "us vs them" mentality

5. **Monitor and Act on Toxic Behavior**
   - Don't let elitism go unchecked
   - Public mockery should have consequences
   - Create safe reporting mechanisms

</div>
